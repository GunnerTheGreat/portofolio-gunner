# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Peers and the developer community.

## Product Purpose
To showcase technical skills, creativity, and unique developer identity. Success means leaving a memorable, striking impression that stands out from typical portfolios.

## Brand Personality
Bold, creative, highly animated. Dark, technical, and cyberpunk/developer-focused.

## Anti-references
- Generic corporate SaaS templates
- Standard minimalist resume-style layouts
- Anything safe, bland, or purely utilitarian

## Design Principles
- **Show, Don't Tell**: Prove technical capabilities through the interface itself (animations, interactions) rather than just listing skills.
- **Atmospheric & Immersive**: Lean heavily into the dark/cyberpunk aesthetic with lighting, motion, and contrast.
- **Unapologetically Bold**: Avoid safe corporate defaults; take risks with typography, layout, and visual effects.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Strict WCAG AA contrast compliance for text (crucial for dark themes).
- Ensure reduced-motion fallbacks (`@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)`) for all heavy animations.
